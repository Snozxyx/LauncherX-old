Your RAM settings.
Setting the RAM allocated to your game, a larger amount will increase your game's performance. Note that it's advisable not to allocate too much RAM as it can cause performance loss in other applications.

- Maximum RAM: Indicates the total amount of RAM allocated to your game. It's recommended not to exceed 50% of the total RAM on your PC for proper functioning. If your computer has more than 8GB of RAM, we recommend allocating 4GB. If your computer has 4GB or less, do not allocate more than 2GB.

- Minimum RAM: Indicates the minimum amount of RAM allocated when your game starts. It's recommended not to allocate more than 3GB for this value. The typical value is 1GB.
